Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how bjshoe.app ("we", "us", "the site") handles information when you play the free online blackjack game hosted here.

What we collect

bjshoe is a single-page browser game. We do not require accounts, and we do not collect names, email addresses, payment information, or any other personal identifiers directly from you.

The only data the game itself stores is kept locally in your browser via localStorage, on your own device:

  • A randomly generated player ID and nickname (e.g. "LuckyAce42")
  • Your current chip balance and last-bet amount
  • Your selected game settings (deck count, animation speed, sound preferences)
  • Your last-used mode and room code

This data never leaves your browser. Clearing your browser storage removes it entirely.

Cookies and third-party advertising

This site is served free of charge and supported by advertising. We use Google AdSense to display ads. AdSense and its partners (including Google) use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites
  • Measure ad performance
  • Provide non-personalized ads in regions where personalized advertising is restricted

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and other sites on the Internet. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

You can also opt out of third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

EU / EEA / UK users

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, Google obtains user consent for advertising cookies in compliance with applicable laws. You may withdraw this consent at any time via the methods described above.

Children's privacy

bjshoe is intended for users 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has used this site, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

Virtual chips have no real-world value

The in-game chips on bjshoe are virtual, free, and have no monetary value. They cannot be purchased, traded, redeemed, or withdrawn. This site does not facilitate real-money gambling.
